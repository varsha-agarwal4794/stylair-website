How it works

Three steps to effortless style.

01

Photograph your clothes

Snap a photo of each item. Stylair's AI instantly removes the background, identifies the category, color, and style — and catalogs it automatically. No manual tagging needed.

02

Describe your mood

Type how you want to feel — "date night, effortlessly cool" or "first day at a new job." Stylair understands natural language and considers today's weather automatically.

03

Wear with confidence

Get three curated outfit suggestions from your actual wardrobe — with an explanation of why each look works. Save favourites, plan ahead, and never open your wardrobe unsure again.
